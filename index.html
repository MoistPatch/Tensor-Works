Who We Are

Australia's International
Fertiliser Commodity Trader

Tensor Works is an Australian-registered international commodity trader and importer/exporter specialising in the procurement and supply of agricultural fertiliser products from global markets.

Operating from Melbourne, Victoria, we connect Australian farmers, co-operatives, and distributors with competitive, quality-assured fertiliser supply from the world's leading production regions — including the Middle East, North Africa, Eastern Europe, and Asia.

Our enquiry process is designed to be straightforward and non-binding, allowing buyers to register their interest and intended volumes to help us secure competitive pricing and allocations from international suppliers on your behalf.
